About Us Page

After chatting about a number of real-world issues, we settled on creating something to address environmental issues. One of our members has an interest in urban planning, and an aspect of that is how current typical suburbs with sprawling lawns directly inhibit biodiversity due to monocultural grass lawns.

We realized that online resources regarding that topic were scattered and text-heavy. Because of this, we decided to develop Decolawnize.
